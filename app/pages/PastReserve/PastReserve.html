                                e-mail id, website and on notice board from time to time. Do not submit any document to accounts
                                department without acknowledgement including scholarship forms.
                                
                            6. All category students who have paid fees as per categor y during their F.Y., S.Y., T.Y. & B.Tech but
                                did not submit the Scholarship / Freeship Forms for whatever reason, are instructed to pay the remaining fees i.e.
                                Difference of Open Category Fee and Category Fee.
                            
                            7. All year down B. Tech Students who have pa id their four year fee in full & M. Tech Students who
